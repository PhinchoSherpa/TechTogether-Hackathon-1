Our Mission

We are dedicated to help connect women from all across the computer science or engineering field.We want to ensure that young women find their passion for tech then we want to help them turn their passions into a reality by building a network of associates.

What We Do

1.  

Explore hundreds of workshops

Hosted by thousands of professionals all over New York City, our workshops will immerse students in first hand experiences of various roles. Students will come to understand how its like to work in a specific field and receive valuable advice from professionals.

2.  

Network with countless professionals

Students can access a myriad of networking opportunities with countless industry professionals. They can learn about their jobs and experiences working in their industry. Networking can provide valuable connections and pathways to future jobs or learning opportunities.

3.  

Shadow experts and gain insight

Find out what a day at work is like in the life of accomplished workers across a wide array of different fields by signing up to shadow people currently employed in professional settings. Students will learn how these people navigate their work environment and apply schlolarly knowledge to the workplace.

4.  

Expand Your Knowledge through Media

Using our wide database on career options and details, we have curated an expansive and informative set of media content for you to browse at will. With this, students can freely broaden their knowledge of careers and go down routes they might've never even considered before.
